What is Vibration Analysis?

In today's world, vibration analysis has evovled immensely from the yesteryear of balancing fans and finding "bad bearings".

Through proper initial vibration analysis set-up, early detection of bearing anomalies can be made and adjusted, corrected, or replaced which improves the longevity, lowers operating costs, and has a positive impact on machine reliability.

Bearing anomalies, faulty machine installations, misalignments, imbalance, resonance, flow turbulence, incorrect machine assembly, motor electrical anomalies, and improper lubrication are just a few of the many problems vibration analysis can detect as an early warning. This is valuable information to you, the machine owner, that can be corrected at your convenience and with minimal negative impact to your operation.

Further comprehensive programs of regular vibration monitoring of your machinery, while it's in operation, will allow trending which helps to identify problems when they arise. Many problems can be resolved at minimal cost to operations and reduced machine downtime.
